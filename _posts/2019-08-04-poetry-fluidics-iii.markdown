## Fluidics III




Ego,




Power,




Political




Influence,




Hedonism,




Flow inward




To be absorbed,




And then to rot —




 In the stench of the grave.




Empathy,

Human Kindness,

Creative Energy,

Flow outward —

Propagating heart-to-heart,


Soul-to-soul,




Forever.
